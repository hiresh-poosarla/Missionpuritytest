Definition of Purity Test:

"A purity test is a self-graded survey that assesses the participants' supposed degree of innocence in worldly matters (sex, drugs, deceit, and other activities assumed to be vices), generally on a percentage scale with 100% being the most and 0% being the least pure. Online purity tests were among the earliest of Internet memes, popular on Usenet beginning in the early 1980s. However, similar types of tests circulated under various names long before the existence of the Internet." - Wikipedia


About the Nice Purity Test

Unlike the traditional purity test, the Nice Purity Test scores the most pure a 0/100 and the least pure a 100/100. Simply put, the Nice Purity Test tells you how many checkboxes you clicked. Many of the questions are common to other purity test. However, the Nice Purity test uses questions more taylored to high school and college students. Additionally, we added a few fun questions inbetween.


Instructions:

Simply click on the checkboxes that apply to you. At the end submit your responce and get a score. As you grow up, take the test again and compare your score. None of the topics in this test are supported or recommended. This is merely a way to illustrate your purity.
